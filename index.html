COMMON NAME:

Neem Tree

BOTANICAL NAME:

Azadirachta indica

SIZE AND SHAPE:

Neem trees are medium to large, reaching heights of 15-20 meters, and can grow up to 30-35 meters under favourable conditions.

MEDICINAL PROPERTIES:

Neem is known for its antimicrobial and antibacterial effects. These properties play a huge role in boosting immunity.

  • Treats Acne
  • Nourishes Skin
  • Treats Fungal Infections
  • Increases immunity
  • Used as insect and mosquito repellent
  • Prevents gastrointestinal diseases
  • Treats wounds
  • Reduces dandruff
  • Exfoliates skin
  • It is also a component in some toothpastes and mouthwashes, especially in the Indian subcontinent, and young twigs are used directly as crude toothbrushes in rural areas.
  • Neem oil, bark, and leaves are unsafe for consumption by pregnant women and can cause miscarriage.
